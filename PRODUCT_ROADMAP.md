# 💧 WaterTracker: Product Feature Roadmap

## 🎯 Product Vision
To transform urban water logistics from a fragmented, manual, and untrusted process into a transparent, data-driven, and efficient digital ecosystem. We aim to empower Apartment Management Committees (Buyers) with accountability and scale Water Tanker Vendors (Sellers) through high-quality lead generation.

---

## 🏗️ Strategic Pillars
1. **Transparency:** Eliminating fraud through digital proof-of-delivery (QR/Barcode).
2. **Accountability:** Enabling rating and review systems for vendors to ensure quality and pricing integrity.
3. **Efficiency:** Automating the "Audit" process for Management Committees (MC).
4. **Marketplace Connectivity:** Creating a two-sided network effect between supply (Vendors) and demand (Apartments).

---

## 🗺️ The 3-Phase Roadmap

### 🚀 Phase 1: The "Utility & Audit" Phase (MVP)
**Goal:** Solve the "Audit" and "Manual Entry" problem for Apartment Admins.
**Focus:** High-utility, low-friction tools to capture data.

*   **[ ] QR/Barcode Entry System:** Single-tap scanner for drivers/admins to log tanker arrival.
*   **[ ] Digital Logbook:** Automated daily/monthly/yearly views of tanker arrivals.
*   **[ ] Expenditure Dashboard:** Auto-calculated monthly spendable reports per vendor.
*   **[ ] Offline-First Architecture:** Ensure data capture is possible even during network outages.
*   **[ ] Push Notifications:** Alerts for Admin/MC members when a tanker is scanned.
*   **[ ] PDF Export:** Generating monthly summary reports for MC meetings.

### 🛡️ Phase 2: The "Trust & Reputation" Phase
**Goal:** Build the "Moat" by introducing qualitative data (Quality & Rating).
**Focus:** Creating the "Trust Layer" that differentiates us from manual logs.

*   **[ ] Vendor Rating System:** Post-delivery rating for "Water Quality," "Timeliness," and "Hygiene."
*   **[ ] Water Quality Logs:** Feature to log pH/TDS/Hardness levels during delivery for transparency.
*   **// [Internal]** *Vendor Verification Badge:* A system to verify legitimate vendors.
*   **[ ] Vendor Dashboard:** A lightweight interface for vendors to view their delivery history and ratings.

### 📈 Phase 3: The "Marketplace" Phase
**Goal:** Facilitate transactions and marketplace dynamics.

*   **[ ] Vendor Lead Generation:** Allowing vendors to "bid" or reach out to apartments based on service areas.
*   **[ ] Integrated Payments:** (Future) In-app payment processing for tanker services.
*   **[ ] Market Analytics:** Providing vendors with data on demand density in specific Bangalore/City neighborhoods.
*   **[ ] Fleet Management Tools:** For larger vendors to track their drivers and tanker capacities.

## 🛠️ Target Personas
1. **The Apartment Admin (The Buyer):** Needs transparency, accountability, and ease of auditing.
2. **The Tanker Driver/Owner (The Seller):** Needs more leads, organized delivery schedules, and a way to prove quality.
